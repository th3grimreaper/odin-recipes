Odin Recipes

  • Day Before Pay Day Fried Rice

    This fried rice uses leftover vegetables and meat--a great way to use leftovers for a dish that can be ready in about 30 minutes.

  • Tuscan Bean Soup (Ribollita)

    This soup is similar to minestrone, but uses beans instead of pasta. A wonderful soup to be enjoyed year-round.

  • All-Day Apple Butter

    This apple butter cooks real slow but is well worth the wait. Depending on the sweetness of the apples used, the amount of sugar may be adjusted to taste.
